# Tea Basics

Tea work in this town is a two-step conversion:

1. Buy a `tea_bundle`
2. Brew it at the `tea_station`
3. Pack it at the `packaging_table`
4. Sell the finished lot to the `general_buyer`

The packaging step requires a `packaging_kit`.
