Workshop operating notes:

- `meal_ingredients` become one `meal_box` at the `meal_prep_table`
- `tea_bundle` plus one `fuel_canister` become one `brewed_tea` at the `tea_station`
- `brewed_tea` plus one `packaging_sleeve` become one `packed_tea` at the `packaging_table`
- `repair_part` becomes one `serviced_device_ticket` at the `repair_bench`

Capacity and recovery notes:

- The starter carry limit is low, so avoid buying junk unless you already have a way to clear it
- The `locker_desk` sells one permanent push-cart rental that expands throughput
- The `discard_bin` can clear a full clearance crate when you need your carry space back
