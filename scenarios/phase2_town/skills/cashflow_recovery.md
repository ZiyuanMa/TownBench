# Cashflow Recovery

When cash is low, the meal loop is the safest restart path:

1. Buy `meal_ingredients`
2. Assemble a `meal_box`
3. Deliver it to the `meal_counter`

Avoid filling your bag with `dusty_trinket` unless you have a reason.

If you already did, clear them through the `goods_buyer` before buying better inputs.
