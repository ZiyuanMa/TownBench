# Capacity Planning

The starter `carry_limit` is small.

- A `clearance_crate` fills four slots immediately
- The `locker_desk` can sell one permanent push-cart rental for 6, raising `carry_limit`
- The `bulk_input_desk` only makes sense after the upgrade
- If junk blocks your next purchase, use the `discard_bin` to clear it

Capacity mistakes are recoverable, but they still cost steps and sometimes money.
