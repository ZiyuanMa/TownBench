# Repair Contracts

Repair work pays well, but the entry ticket is higher:

1. Buy one `repair_part` for 8 at the `parts_bin`
2. Use the `repair_bench` to create one `serviced_device_ticket`
3. Hand the ticket to the `pickup_clerk` for a 15-coin payout

This loop is usually best after one or two cheaper loops have already rebuilt your cash buffer.
