# Service Contracts

Repair work pays more than meal prep, but it starts with a bigger cash commitment.

1. Buy a `repair_part`
2. Finish the job at the `repair_bench`
3. Bring the `serviced_device_ticket` to the `pickup_clerk`

If you cannot afford the part yet, run the meal loop first and come back with more working capital.
