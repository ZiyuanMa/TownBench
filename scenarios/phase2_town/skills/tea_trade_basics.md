# Tea Trade Basics

Tea work needs three inputs:

1. Buy one `tea_bundle`
2. Buy one `fuel_canister`
3. Buy one `packaging_sleeve`
4. Brew at the `tea_station`
5. Pack at the `packaging_table`
6. Sell the `packed_tea` to the `goods_buyer`

The full input cost is 6 and the finished lot sells for 10, so the loop earns 4 before counting time and energy.
