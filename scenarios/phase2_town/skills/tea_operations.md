# Tea Operations

The tea loop is profitable, but it is not a one-click payout.

1. Buy a `tea_bundle`
2. Buy a `fuel_canister`
3. Buy a `packaging_sleeve`
4. Brew at the `tea_station`
5. Pack at the `packaging_table`
6. Sell the `packed_tea` to the `goods_buyer`

The loop only works repeatedly if you keep buying both fuel and sleeves.
